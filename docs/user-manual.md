# User Manual — Teachers, Parents & Students

## Signing in
Open your school's address (e.g. `sunrise.educore.in`), enter the email and password your
school shared with you, and press **Sign in**. Use **Forgot password?** to reset by email.
The theme toggle (moon/sun, top right) switches dark mode; your choice is remembered.

## Teachers
- **Mark attendance**: Attendance → pick class & section → tap each student's status
  (Present is pre-filled) → **Save**. Parents of absentees are notified automatically.
- **Find a student**: use the search bar at the top of any page — name or admission number.
- **Announcements**: items pinned by the principal stay at the top of your dashboard.

## Parents
- **Dashboard** shows today's attendance, pending fees and pinned notices for your child.
- **Fees**: open Fees to see invoices; **Pay now** supports UPI, cards and net banking.
  Receipts are emailed and stay downloadable under each invoice.

## Students
- Dashboard shows your timetable, homework due and exam results as they're published.

## Trouble signing in?
Check that you're on **your school's** address (the part before `.educore.in`), then contact
the school office — they can reset your account from the admin console.
